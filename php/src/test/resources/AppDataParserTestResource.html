      Product Description
      The Race Rally is the best 3d arcade racing game features day or night rainy mode with lights on or off and six different camera views. Race along with other artificial intelligence (AI) cars for the desired number of laps assisted with back camera view mirror and win!

      Key features;
      ✓ Beautiful 3D graphics of unique race track & cars
      ✓ Challenge AI opponents
      ✓ Drizzling rain or day mode
      ✓ Turn into night racing with lights on or off
      ✓ Custom choice of Steering, Stick, or Motion control for wheel and throttle
      ✓ Picture-in-Picture(PIP) 2D overview track map
      ✓ Continuous feed - car back camera view mirror
      ✓ Choose from 3 fun game modes Race & Chase, Countdown & Timer
      ✓ 6 diff.camera views including front facing car view (Inside/Outside & Sky views)
      ✓ Collision fire sparks
      ✓ Choice of race car color
      ✓ Unique and absorbing music tracks plus SFX/sound controls
      ✓ Amazingly small footprint of binary size for a quality 3D racing game.

      Choose from three different game modes: Race-n-Chase with AI cars, Countdown, and Timer mode with custom choice of Steering, Stick, or Motion control for wheel and throttle. You can optionally turn off engine (SFX) or music sound.

      Chill out your racing experience with an option to turn on rain shower and lights. Enjoy the rally action packed ride!

      Please note: We highly recommend dual core processors like kindle fire, motorolo photon 4g, samsung galaxy tablet with android version supporting open GL 2.0+ for 3D lighting and shading etc.

      3.0 out of 5 stars Guys... gals... seriously... trust me... March 16, 2013
      By 3n19m470
      Amazon Verified Purchase
      Only a very new gamer or a young child say, 10, 11 or younger, would like this game. There's nothing too wrong with it, per se... It's just very very basic.

      1 track only.

      1 car only, although you may change the color... Maybe red is faster, I dunno lol

      There is no way to upgrade your car.

      There are no powerups, coins, speed boost, or ANYTHING.

      This game is simply one track, one car, a gas pedal, a brake pedal, (and another pedal that can only be the clutch??? but there is no manual shift mode...unless I missed something.) and three game modes, Race mode against the computer cars (there's only like two, so three cars racing if you include yourself, not exactly nail biting competition...), Time Mode, where you try to complete the race by yourself and see how fast you can do it. Then countdown mode, which is like time mode except the clock runs backwards and when it hits zero you lose.

      So, yeah, very extremely basic game. So just don't get it unless you are prepared for the ramifications...
